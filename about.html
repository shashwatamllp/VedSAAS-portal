VedSAAS kya hai

“Vedic Intelligence + Scientific AI”, developed by Shashwatam Eco-Chic Creations LLP. Goal:

“Bharat ke sabhi logon ko apni bhasha me, apni sanskriti ke hisaab se ek AI dena jo unka teacher, advisor, aur saathi bane.”

🧠 Core Architecture

VedSAAS ka brain do engines par kaam karta hai. Beech me Brain Controller jo decide karta hai kis query ko kis engine me bhejna hai.

EngineRoleFunction
Engine 1 Front reasoning brain User se baat (Hindi/English/Hinglish), intent detect, quick answers; wiki, intent.json, universal.json, main.json, tokens.db se fetch.
Engine 2 Deep knowledge brain Internet/docs/APIs se research; self-learning & background indexing (e.g., granth, gov data, RSS, Wikipedia).

📚 Knowledge System

  1. main.json — short definitions & quick facts
  2. universal.json — disambiguation & topic options
  3. tokens.db — detailed semantic knowledge (SQLite)

Plus: web_learn_and_answer() se auto-learning & background healing.

🧩 Modules

  • Gurukul — NCERT aligned education
  • Krishi — farmer-first guidance
  • Guardian — parental & safety controls
  • Granth Pack — Sanātani texts
  • Databank Sync — S3 mirroring (index/latest)
